Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out our quote request forms
  • Contact us via phone or email
  • Request a site survey
  • Sign up for our newsletter

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, postcode, and details about your project requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide you with quotes and estimates
  • Schedule site surveys and installations
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To trusted partners who assist us in providing services
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
