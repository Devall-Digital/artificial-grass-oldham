How We Work

Step 1: Request Free Quotes

Fill out our simple quote request form with your details and garden requirements. We'll need basic information like your name, contact details, postcode, and garden size.

Step 2: We Connect You with Installers

We'll connect you with our network of trusted artificial grass installers. These installers are:

  • ✓ Fully qualified and experienced
  • ✓ Insured and registered
  • ✓ Serving your local area
  • ✓ Committed to competitive pricing

Installers will contact you directly with personalised quotes based on your property and requirements.

Step 3: Compare and Choose

Receive quotes from multiple installers and compare prices, warranties, and service offerings. Choose the installer that best meets your needs and budget.
